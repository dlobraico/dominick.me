Projects

This is a non-exhaustive list of some of the hobby projects that I've been working on lately. Much of the code for this work and other projects is on Github.

Linus is a web application for managing the steps of the publication process, from story brainstorming to copy-editing to publishing.
The Daily Sophist is a news publication at the University of Chicago. I co-founded The Sophist with my friend Adam in 2011 and wrote the Rails app that runs the site.
The Chicago Shady Dealer is a campus humor publication at the University of Chicago. The latest incarnation of the publication's website is a Sinatra app pulling articles from a Linus instance that we use to manage our publication process.
